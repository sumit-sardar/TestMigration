Privacy Policy (Protecting Your Privacy)

When you register for any of our conferences, request information about our products and services, or communicate with us for any other reason via online form submission, we add your e-mail address to our electronic mailing list so that, from time to time, we can send you information about new features, additions, and changes to our Web site. Any additional information that you provide will be used only to process your registration or respond to your request. We use "cookies" only to help you navigate our Web site. We do not use cookies to gather information about you, nor can we access, collect, or change any information on your computer through the use of our cookies. If you would like to confirm the accuracy of any information that we may have collected from you, please write to privacy@ctb.com If you have questions about our use of any information you provide to us, you may contact the Privacy Council at CTB/McGraw-Hill, 20 Ryan Ranch Road, Monterey, CA 93940-5703. Please be assured that we take your concerns about privacy quite seriously, and we intend to take every reasonable effort to protect it. To that end, The McGraw-Hill Companies has developed a comprehensive privacy policy. If you'd like more information about our use of customer data, please review the McGraw-Hill Education Privacy Policy.
